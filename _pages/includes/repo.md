[Open-AISP: An open-source AI-ISP pipeline framework for beginners](https://github.com/coolsyn2000/Open-AISP)

[**[Github]**](https://github.com/coolsyn2000/Open-AISP) [**[Docs]**](https://www.coolsyn.top/Open-AISP/) [**[中文文档]**](https://www.coolsyn.top/Open-AISP/zh/) ![GitHub stars](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/coolsyn2000/Open-AISP?style=social)
- A beginner-friendly AI-ISP pipeline framework covering realistic raw image degradation simulation, neural reconstruction, and image enhancement.
- Current modules include raw simulation (`raw-sim`) and multi-frame joint denoising/demosaicing (`JDD`), with HDR synthesis, learning-based tonemapping, and diffusion-based post-enhancement on the roadmap.
