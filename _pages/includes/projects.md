## 📷 Computational Photography
<div class='paper-box'><div class='paper-box-image'><div><div class="badge">Github</div><img src='images/Open-AISP.png' alt="Open-AISP result" width="500" height="300"></div></div>
<div class='paper-box-text' markdown="1">

[Open-AISP: An open-source AI-ISP pipeline framework for beginners](https://github.com/coolsyn2000/Open-AISP)

[**[Github]**](https://github.com/coolsyn2000/Open-AISP) [**[Docs]**](https://www.coolsyn.top/Open-AISP/) [**[中文文档]**](https://www.coolsyn.top/Open-AISP/zh/) ![GitHub stars](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/coolsyn2000/Open-AISP?style=social)
- A beginner-friendly and toy-level AI-ISP pipeline framework covering realistic raw image degradation simulation, neural ISP pipeline reconstruction, and image post-enhancement with generative prior.
- Current modules include raw simulation raw-sim and multi-frame joint denoising/demosaicing (JDD), with multi-frame HDR fusion (HDR-fusion), learning-based tonemapping(AITM), and diffusion-based post-enhancement (DiffIPE) on the roadmap.
